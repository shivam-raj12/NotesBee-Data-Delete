Account & Data Deletion

Method 1: In-App Deletion

The fastest way to delete your account and all associated data is through the app:

1. Open the app and Login.
2. Navigate to Settings.
3. Tap the Delete Account button.

Method 2: Manual Request

If you can no longer access the app, you may request that your account and all personal data be permanently deleted by contacting support:

srdevelopsteam@gmail.com
Requirement: To verify ownership, you must send this email from your registered email address.
Once the request is processed, your account, personal information, and all associated data will be purged from our systems according to our privacy policy.
